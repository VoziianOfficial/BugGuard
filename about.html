Mission

Pest concerns are easier to compare when the request is clear first.

BugGuard exists to help homeowners move from vague pest concerns to a more organized provider-matching request. The platform focuses on categories, signs, locations, and comparison prompts — not direct pest treatment claims.

Clear service categories

Responsible information

Details worth noting before choosing a provider option.

Do not ignore recurring signs

Repeated activity may be useful context when local providers evaluate possible next steps.

Check entry points

Gaps, vents, doors, storage zones, and garage edges can help describe where activity appears.

Pay attention to food areas

Kitchen and pantry details can help organize the request around indoor pest activity.

Watch fabrics and pet areas

For flea-related concerns, describe fabrics, carpets, bedding, and pet spaces carefully.

Be careful around outdoor nests

Visible stinging insect nests should be described without attempting risky DIY removal.

Ask the right questions

Provider questions may include evaluation scope, possible options, timing, and follow-up.
