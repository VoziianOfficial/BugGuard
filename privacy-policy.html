Overview

BugGuard is an independent pest control provider-matching platform. The website helps users submit request information and compare local provider options for pest-related concerns. BugGuard does not directly perform pest control work, inspections, treatments, extermination, nest removal, chemical applications, repairs, or medical services.

By using this website, submitting a request, or interacting with site features, you agree that information may be processed as described in this Privacy Policy.

Platform role: BugGuard may help organize request information for provider matching, but local providers are responsible for their own services, pricing, availability, evaluations, and outcomes.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you submit directly through forms or links on this website. This may include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Service category selected, such as rodents, spiders, fleas, general pests, or stinging insects.
  • Details about the pest concern, location of activity, urgency, timing, and related notes.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and basic usage data.
  • Cookie or local storage preferences used to remember your cookie banner choice.

Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information that is not necessary for a provider-matching request.

How We Use Information

BugGuard may use submitted information to operate the website, respond to requests, organize provider-matching details, improve user experience, and maintain basic site security.

Examples of use

  • To understand the service category and pest concern you submitted.
  • To contact you about your request or related provider-matching steps.
  • To help organize request details so relevant provider options can be compared.
  • To maintain website functionality, improve navigation, and troubleshoot errors.
  • To comply with applicable legal, safety, or platform obligations.

BugGuard does not use submitted request information to provide direct pest treatment, inspection, diagnosis, extermination, or medical advice.

Information Sharing

Information may be shared when necessary to support provider matching, operate website systems, comply with legal obligations, or protect platform integrity.

  • With service providers or systems that help operate the website and process requests.
  • With potential local provider options when needed to support your request.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • When necessary to protect rights, safety, security, or prevent misuse of the website.

Local providers are independent from BugGuard. Their own privacy practices, service terms, pricing, availability, and outcomes may differ.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request through the website. You may also contact BugGuard using the email listed below for privacy-related questions.

  • You can avoid submitting optional information in request forms.
  • You can clear browser cookies or local storage through your browser settings.
  • You can contact us about privacy questions using the contact details on this page.

Some website features may not work correctly if cookies, local storage, or basic browser functionality is disabled.

Security

BugGuard uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information submitted through the website. However, no website, online form, email, storage system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

You are responsible for submitting accurate information and avoiding unnecessary sensitive details in request messages.

Important limitation

BugGuard does not guarantee provider response time, provider availability, service quality, inspection outcome, treatment result, extermination result, nest removal result, pricing, or pest-control success.
