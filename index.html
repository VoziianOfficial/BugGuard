Before you submit

Details that make a pest request easier to compare.

You do not need to know the exact pest type. Start with what you noticed, where it appeared, and how often it happens.

Where signs appear

Kitchen, pantry, attic, garage, bedroom, exterior wall, garden area, roofline, or storage space.

How often it happens

One-time activity, repeated movement, seasonal signs, night sounds, or activity returning after cleaning.

What you noticed

Droppings, webs, insects, nests, scratching sounds, bites, fabric concerns, or entry gaps.

Questions to ask

Ask local providers about evaluation steps, service scope, preparation, follow-up, pricing, and limitations.

Responsible request guidance

Details that may help local providers understand the situation.

Pest concerns can be different from home to home. These prompts help you describe what you see without assuming a treatment result.

Food areas and indoor activity

Note whether activity appears near kitchens, pantries, bins, sinks, or stored food.

Entry points and recurring signs

Look for repeated activity around gaps, doors, garage edges, vents, or storage zones.

Outdoor stinging insects

Visible nests near eaves, rooflines, trees, or outdoor paths may require careful provider evaluation.

Fabric, pet, and flea concerns

Describe bites, fabrics, carpets, bedding, and pet areas without making medical assumptions.

Indoor comfort and recurring activity

Share whether activity is new, seasonal, repeated after temporary fixes, or spreading.

Provider questions before choosing

Ask local providers about evaluation steps, possible options, follow-up expectations, and service scope.

Questions before you start

Clear answers about BugGuard.

Is this a direct pest control company?

No. BugGuard is an independent provider-matching platform. It does not directly perform pest inspections, treatments, extermination, nest removal, chemical applications, or repair work.

How does provider matching work?

You describe the issue, choose a service category, and use the request details to compare local provider options that may fit the situation.

Can I compare local pest control options?

Yes. The platform is designed to help homeowners organize information and review provider options before choosing the fit they prefer.

What pest issues can I submit?

You can start with general pests, rodents, ants and cockroaches, spiders, wasps and hornets, fleas, or mixed recurring signs.

Does the platform guarantee treatment results?

No. BugGuard does not guarantee provider results, pricing, availability, inspection outcomes, treatment success, or same-day service.

Can I start with an urgent pest concern?

You can submit urgent details in the request. Local providers may review availability and possible next steps, but BugGuard does not guarantee response timing.
