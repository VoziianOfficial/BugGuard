Overview

BugGuard uses limited cookies, local storage, and similar browser technologies to support essential website functionality, remember cookie choices, improve navigation, and maintain basic user experience.

BugGuard is an independent provider-matching platform. Cookie use on this website does not mean BugGuard directly performs pest control work, inspections, extermination, treatment, nest removal, chemical applications, repairs, or medical services.

Simple storage: The cookie banner uses local storage to remember whether you accepted or cancelled the cookie notice.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Local storage is another browser-based storage method that can remember basic preferences on a website.

These technologies can help websites remember settings, maintain basic functionality, improve page behavior, and understand general usage patterns.

  • Cookies may be placed by the website or by third-party tools used on the website.
  • Local storage may remember simple preferences, such as cookie notice choices.
  • Browser settings usually allow you to delete or block cookies and local storage.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

BugGuard may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your cookie banner choice.
  • To support website navigation, menus, forms, and interactive sections.
  • To help load third-party libraries used for icons, animations, and interface behavior.
  • To improve basic site performance, layout behavior, and visitor experience.
  • To support security, debugging, and website maintenance where applicable.

The website is designed as a static provider-matching site. Any form information you choose to submit is handled according to the Privacy Policy.

Essential Storage

Some browser storage is necessary for the website to function as intended. For example, the cookie banner uses a local storage key to avoid showing the same notice repeatedly after you make a choice.

Examples

  • Remembering whether the cookie notice was accepted or cancelled.
  • Supporting interactive menus, accordions, swipers, and mobile navigation behavior.
  • Maintaining basic site usability across pages.

If you block or delete cookies and local storage, the website may still work, but some preferences or interface behaviors may reset.

Third-Party Tools

BugGuard may load third-party front-end libraries or resources to support the visual experience of the website. These may include icon libraries, animation libraries, slider libraries, fonts, or other static assets.

  • Icon libraries may be used to display interface icons.
  • Animation libraries may be used to create scroll-based effects.
  • Slider libraries may be used for FAQ or content carousel behavior.
  • External resource providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, or request metadata.

Third-party tools may use their own cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. BugGuard does not control third-party privacy or cookie practices.

Your Cookie Controls

You can control cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete stored data, block certain cookies, or receive notifications before cookies are stored.

  • You can clear cookies and site data in your browser settings.
  • You can block some or all cookies depending on browser options.
  • You can clear local storage to reset the cookie notice choice.
  • You can choose not to submit forms if you do not want to provide request details.

Blocking storage may affect cookie notice behavior, interactive menus, form behavior, or other site features.

Provider-matching limitation

Cookie preferences do not affect BugGuard’s platform role. BugGuard remains an independent provider-matching website and does not directly perform pest control work.
